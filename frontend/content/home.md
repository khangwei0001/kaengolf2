## Get to know us (About KAEN Performance Composites) 
- 
KAEN Performance Composite is not only a brand, but a network of golf enthusiasts and industry professionals from around the world. It has evolved from a casual conversation in early 2020 amid the global pandemic and lock down, into the products we have to offer today.  It is a brand of passion, inspiration, and perseverance.

 

KAEN Performance Composite golf shafts represent the collaboration of leading industry experts, designers, and manufacturing facilities from around the world based on data input from social, amateur and professional players. It is the melting pot of the past, present and future of the golf industry.

### H.D.C (High Density Carbon)
- Engineered with ultra-high-strength carbon fibers packed at exceptional density, KAEN High Density Fiber reinforces the shaft’s structure from core to surface. This advanced material increases torsional rigidity, minimizes unwanted deformation, and preserves shaft geometry under maximum load. 

### A.T.T (Active Torque Technology)
- We have adopted a unique torque setting based on data collected from professional fitters around the world, and the shaft responds to various types of swings, providing stable control and direction.

### H.C.L (Hybrid Carbon Layering)
- High-elasticity carbon material is woven at specific angles and in specific orders to stabilize direction.
​
### Descending Parallel Tip Design
- Another major feature is that all shafts, from drivers to irons, use a descending design (single shaft). By adjusting the stiffness with tip cuts, it is possible to derive the optimal feeling for your swing. The advantage of the "single shaft for irons" is outstanding, and while constant weight design shafts (designed by club) change design for each club, by using the same shaft for all clubs, it is possible to swing without changing the feeling.
